About us

Project Synopsis

Covid Yoddha is a system built on Flask Framework which focuses on the problems faced by the areas which are highly affected by the Covid-19.

Targeted Users are citizens, people under quarantine in hotspots, government officials managing hotspots, NGOs, healthcare profeesionals and authorities.

Our system provides a platform not just for doctors, nurses and medical assistants but also regular citizens to volunteer their services to help the government during this difficult time. Citizens can register, through the system as volunteers in their neighboorhood and help out. The system also keeps the track of the volunteers.

The proposed system would able to potentially predict upcoming hotspots and would alert the remaining citizens in hotspot area as well as the government authorities to seal the area immediately.

It also serves as a platform to register as a blood donor and notifies authorities about areas with enough donors so that a mobile blood donation drive can be conducted.

The system can be used by citizens in sealed areas, that are legally prohibited from stepping out of their homes to request essential goods that they require which can later be delivered to them at their doorstep through volunteers.

Charitable citizens will also find a page directing them to the PM-CARES fund portal and other charity organizations portals.
